Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

This privacy policy describes how the SMS Assistant tool operated by Jeffrey Wentworth / JW Accounting RI ("we," "us," or "our") handles information submitted through our personal SMS messaging service.

What this tool does

This is a private, personal productivity tool. It receives SMS messages sent by the registered user and saves them as tasks, notes, or expense records in a private Airtable database. It is not a public service and is not accessible to anyone other than the account holder.

Information we collect

When a text message is sent to this service, we collect:

  • The content of the text message
  • The date and time the message was sent
  • The sender's phone number (used only to verify the message came from the registered user)

How we use this information

The information collected is used solely to log tasks, notes, and expenses into a private database for the account holder's personal use. We do not use this information for marketing, advertising, or any other purpose.

Who has access to this information

Only the account holder has access to the stored data. We do not sell, share, or disclose any information to third parties.

Third-party services

This tool uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Twilio — routes SMS messages to our server. Subject to Twilio's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic — processes message text to classify and extract information. Subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Airtable — stores the logged data. Subject to Airtable's Privacy Policy.

Data retention

Data is retained in the account holder's Airtable base until manually deleted by the account holder.

Opting out

To stop using this service, simply stop sending messages to the associated phone number. No further data will be collected. To delete existing data, the account holder may delete records directly from their Airtable base.
